# Quantum Framework - Theoretical Foundation

## Origin

The DSV (Decompose-Suspend-Validate) framework was born from applying quantum physics metaphors to reasoning. Quantum Lens extends this: where DSV validates claims by holding them in superposition, Quantum Lens GENERATES new perspectives through quantum-inspired cognitive instruments.

**Critical distinction**: DSV validates. Quantum Lens generates. Same vocabulary, opposite purpose.

## Core Principles

### 1. Superposition as Valid Output
Traditional analysis forces premature resolution. Quantum Lens treats ambiguity as structured and actionable (inspired by Superpositional Belief research). Multiple interpretations coexist until a specific goal forces collapse.

**Implementation**: Agents list 2-4 readings per atom, tagged by type. No ranking. No "best interpretation." The superposition IS the output.

### 2. Entanglement as Dependency Mapping
Concepts are linked. Changing one forces changes in others. Three entanglement models serve as equal analytical instruments:
- **Linked Pairs**: If A changes, B MUST change. Maps rigid dependencies.
- **Cascading Flips**: One change triggers a chain reaction. Maps dynamic consequences.
- **Absence Entanglement**: The presence of X implies the absence of Y. Maps exclusion patterns.

### 3. Interference as Signal Detection
When independent analysts examine the same input, their agreements (constructive) and disagreements (destructive) reveal structure:
- **Constructive interference**: High confidence signal. Independent convergence.
- **Destructive interference**: Breakthrough candidate. The disagreement IS the insight.
- **Isomorphisms**: Same deep pattern, different surfaces. Structural truth.

### 4. Collapse as Decision Instrument
Ambiguity is collapsed deliberately, not accidentally. The Killer Question is a measurement device - its answer forces the highest-entropy superposition to resolve.

**Implementation**: Goal-conditioned collapse. "If your goal is X, collapse toward these insights."

### 5. Tunneling as Frame-Breaking
"Impossible" barriers exist within a frame. Change the frame, and the barrier vanishes. Every dead end contains a tunnel if you strip the assumptions that define the walls.

### 6. Anti-Convergence by Structure
Sonnet agents tend to converge even when instructed not to. Quantum Lens enforces divergence through STRUCTURAL constraints, not just instructions:
- Each lens has a computationally distinct cognitive mode
- Output formats force unique sections (Absence Map vs. Impossibility Register)
- Voice markers are hard constraints on language patterns
- Atom-level tracking ensures lenses reference different primary evidence
- Convergence = suspicion, not confidence (from NeurIPS Multi-LLM Debate research)

## Cognitive Modes (Not Personas)

Each lens is grounded in a computationally distinct cognitive strategy, NOT an expert persona:
- **Negative space cognition** (Void Reader) - autistic detail-focus on absence
- **Dialectical synthesis** (Paradox Hunter) - ADHD divergent association
- **Holistic integration** (Boundary Dissolver) - right hemisphere dominance
- **Process-oriented cognition** (Temporal Archaeologist) - dynamic systems thinking
- **Fractal/spatial reasoning** (Scale Shifter) - dyslexic-style 3D mapping
- **Inversion reasoning** (Failure Romantic) - pre-mortem, Jacobi's "invert always invert"
- **Cross-domain structural mapping** (Interference Reader) - Gentner's structure-mapping
